About

SpeakOut is a tool for broadcasting speeches at protests. It may be useful in other demonstration and event contexts, too.

SpeakOut is in beta mode. It's built by Elizabeth Ferguson as part of thesis at ITP (Interactive Telecommunications Program) at NYUâ€™s Tisch School of the Arts.

Thank you to Transportation Alternatives, the New York City advocacy group for safer streets, for feedback on this prototype.

Please reach out if you are interested in collaborating on SpeakOut. Collaborations might include testing in new demonstration contexts, or working together on the back-end.
