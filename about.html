About

SpeakOut is a tool for broadcasting speeches at protests. It may be useful in other demonstration and event contexts, too.

SpeakOut is built by Elizabeth Ferguson as part of her thesis at ITP (Interactive Telecommunications Program) at NYUâ€™s Tisch School of the Arts.

The web app is originally designed for Transportation Alternatives, the New York City advocacy group for safer streets.

Please reach out if you are interested in collaborating on SpeakOut. Collaborations could look like live testing in new demonstration or other contexts, or working together on the back-end.
